About Me

My Journey

At 27, I've spent the last 5 years refining my design skills across various industries. My background in fine arts gives me a unique perspective on digital design, blending traditional aesthetics with modern functionality.

I believe in design that serves a purpose - every element should have intention. My minimalist approach comes from this philosophy, stripping away the unnecessary to highlight what truly matters.

When I'm not designing, you can find me sketching in coffee shops, exploring typography, or photographing urban landscapes for inspiration.
